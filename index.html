Zion is a new gen Ride hailing application that aims to provide quick and affordable Rides for consumers by leveraging in-vehicle advertising.

Our innovative approach ensures transparent pricing, better earnings for drivers, and cost-effective advertising solutions for brands seeking engaged audiences.

Who are we

At Zion, we’re reimagining ride-hailing for India.

We believe getting from one place to another should be fast, affordable, and sustainable — not dependent on endless discounts that vanish overnight.

Our platform is built to serve three communities together:

  • Riders get the quickest rides at the lowest fares.
  • Drivers earn more with fair commissions and an extra source of income.
  • Brands reach a truly engaged audience through in-ride advertising.

Unlike traditional ride-hailing apps, Zion isn’t just about rides — it’s about building a model where everyone wins. By combining mobility with in-vehicle advertising, we’re creating a system that keeps fares low, drivers happy, and advertisers remembered.

We’re just getting started, but our mission is clear:

To make ride-hailing sustainable, engaging, and rewarding for everyone!
