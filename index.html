The model

How Zion keeps rides affordable.

Two systems working together — one is what makes Zion different, the other just makes it better.

Core model

In-ride advertising

Every Zion auto carries a screen that plays ads for the length of the ride — a captive, undistracted audience brands actually pay for. That ad revenue gets split: part goes straight to the driver as extra income, part funds lower fares for riders. This is the real reason Zion can charge less than everyone else.

Screens installed in every auto
Ad revenue split with drivers
The rest funds lower fares
Bonus, on top

Monthly driver packages

Instead of losing ~30% to per-ride commission like most platforms, drivers pay one flat monthly fee. It's a small cost that keeps the full fare in the driver's pocket — which means Zion can price rides even lower.

Cab ₹1,500 / month

Who we are

At Zion, we're reimagining ride-hailing for India.

We believe getting from one place to another should be fast, affordable, and sustainable — not dependent on endless discounts that vanish overnight.

Our platform is built to serve three communities together:

  • Riders get the quickest rides at the lowest fares.
  • Drivers earn more with fair commissions and an extra source of income.
  • Brands reach a truly engaged audience through in-ride advertising.

Unlike traditional ride-hailing apps, Zion isn't just about rides — it's about building a model where everyone wins. By combining mobility with in-vehicle advertising, we're creating a system that keeps fares low, drivers happy, and advertisers remembered.

To make ride-hailing sustainable, engaging, and rewarding for everyone.

Cancellations & Refunds

At Zion, we strive to provide our riders with a seamless and affordable travel experience. We understand that plans may change, so here's how our policy works:

Ride Cancellations (Riders)

  • Riders may cancel a ride at any time before the driver has arrived at the pickup location.
  • In certain cases, a nominal cancellation fee may apply to compensate the driver.
  • Cancelling within 2 minutes of booking incurs no fee.

Ride Cancellations (Drivers)

  • Drivers are expected to honor accepted trips. Frequent cancellations may result in warnings, penalties, or suspension.
  • If a driver cancels, riders are not charged any cancellation fee.

Refunds

  • If a ride is not completed due to driver cancellation, technical issues, or other valid reasons, any advance payment is refunded to your original payment method.
  • Refunds typically process within 5–7 business days.
  • No refunds are issued once a ride has been completed.

Disputes

In case of disputes related to fares, cancellations, or payments, riders may raise a ticket via Help & Support in the app. Each case is reviewed by our team, with refunds provided where applicable.
