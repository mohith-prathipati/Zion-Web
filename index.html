About Us

Zion is a new gen Ride hailing application that aims to provide quick and affordable Rides for consumers by leveraging in-vehicle advertising.

We revolutionize the ride-hailing industry by creating a sustainable ecosystem where ad revenue directly reduces ride costs, benefiting riders, drivers, and advertisers alike.

Our innovative approach ensures transparent pricing, better earnings for drivers, and cost-effective advertising solutions for brands seeking engaged audiences.
